Executive Summary · March 2026
Squamish Civic Forum — Master Plan
Built by the community. For the community.
A non-partisan civic organization designed to solve every major gap from three election campaigns — an MP nomination, a federal MP campaign, and a BC MLA campaign. One platform, one direction, one movement for the 2026 Squamish municipal election. This is the V1 implementation of that thinking.

What This Solves

After three campaigns, these were the same problems every time. Squamish Civic Forum is built specifically to fix each one.

⚡
No shared platform
Good people got fired up and scattered in five directions. No coordination, no way to consolidate energy into votes.
📭
No information
Residents voted blind. No council scorecards, no plain-language summaries, no fact packages on major decisions.
🏃
Candidates ran alone
Good people didn't run because they had no support, no research team, no base behind them.
🙉
Nobody was listening
Issues that mattered to hockey parents, old Squamish families, hunters, renters — never made it into the room.
🔥
Always reactive
By the time people got frustrated, decisions were already made. No early warning system, no proactive mobilization.
🎯
Squamish Civic Forum fixes all five.
One platform. One direction. One movement — built before the campaign starts, not during it.

Mission & Purpose

"For busy people who care about Squamish — we do the research so you don't have to. Real data. Real issues. Considered recommendations. 5 minutes to stay informed."

🔍
Research-Driven
Track council votes, attend meetings, dig into the facts. Real information — not spin.
⚖️
Non-Partisan
No party lines. No ideology. Just Squamish residents who care about getting things right.
📣
Community-First
We listen before we speak. Your issues shape our platform — not the other way around.
🗳️
Candidate-Ready
Vet candidates, publish questionnaires, and help the community make the best choice.
