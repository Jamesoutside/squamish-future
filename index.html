About Us

Who we are

Squamish Future is a grassroots community group built by locals who believe all of Squamish deserves a seat at the table — not just the loudest voices. We're not left or right. We're not any one neighbourhood or any one crowd. We're Squamish.

Our goal is simple: more engaged voters, more informed residents, and a community that makes decisions together — based on real data and shared values, not spin.

🏔️

Local First

Squamish residents, for Squamish residents.

📊

Evidence-Based

We cite sources. We check facts. We get it right.

🤲

Volunteer-Powered

No corporate money. Built by people who care.

🔓

Transparent

Our decisions, our finances, our reasoning — open.

How We're Structured — Two Streams

🌐 Public Stream
  • Issues platform and research
  • Council scorecards and vote tracking
  • Candidate questionnaires (published)
  • Community events and forums
  • Newsletter and social media
🔒 Private Working Group
  • Candidate vetting and support
  • Strategy and coordination
  • Resource allocation decisions
  • Confidential candidate conversations
