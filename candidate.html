The Process

How the candidate pipeline works

We don't run candidates. We support the right ones. Here's how it works from expression of interest to election night.

1

Expression of Interest

You fill out the private form below. Completely confidential — just between you and our vetting team.

2

Private Conversation

We reach out for a 1-on-1 conversation. No commitment needed — just an honest talk about fit, values, and readiness.

3

Vetting & Scoring

Our team assesses community alignment, platform fit, and credibility using our published scoring framework.

4

Support & Endorsement

Endorsed candidates get our full public support: talking points, research, base access, event invitations, and community backing.

What You Get

What We Look For

Values that get our support

We're non-partisan — we don't care about party labels. We care about these things.

🏔️

Community-First

You make decisions based on what's best for Squamish residents — not developers, not ideology, not career advancement.

📊

Evidence-Based

You believe in facts. You change your mind when evidence changes. You cite sources.

🤝

Accountable

You show up. You vote. You explain your decisions. You respond to constituents.

🔓

Transparent

No hidden agendas. No backroom deals. You tell people what you're doing and why.
